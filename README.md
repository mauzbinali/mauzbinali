### 🖐 SignSpeak AI — Sign Language Reader & Learning App

An AI-style sign language learning and hand sign reader app built with Flutter. It includes a beautiful learning interface, sign library, camera-based recognition concept, animated AI scanning UI, hand skeleton/landmark visualization concept, practice mode, and common hand signs for beginners.

**Signs include:** ME, YOU, YES, NO, SORRY, THANK YOU, PLEASE, EAT, DRINK, MORE, WHERE, PHONE, MOTHER, FATHER, MILK, STOP, OKAY, GOOD JOB, NOT GOOD, I LOVE YOU, CALL ME, PEACE, POINT, WATER, and ROCK ON.

**Tech:** Flutter, Dart, Camera Integration, AI-style UI, Custom Assets, Text-to-Speech Concept
